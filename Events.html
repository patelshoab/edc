Hackathon: InnovateToInspire Hackathon

June 08th, 2019

Malviya incubation and innovation centre, MNIT, Jaipur About the Hackathon: The main objective of #InnovateToINSPIRE Hackathon is to identify top workable business solutions around the data being captured through EESL's programmes like 'National E-Mobility Programme', 'Smart Meter National Programme' and 'Street Lighting National Programme'. These Hackathons are being organised to accelerate the entire ecosystem and find data analytics cases that can be adopted to enhance consumer experience and develop business models to commercialise this database.

Inauguration Program

Sep 04th, 2018

EDC have arranged an Inauguration program of Entrepreneurship Development Cell for students of Government College of Engineering Aurangabad for making entrepreneurial culture among students and want to make students aware about entrepreneurship. Sanjay shidhye, Suresh Shakkarwar, Ashutosh Navendar were present as chief guest in Program. EDC have arranged an open discussion about Startup, incubation, investment and all with first generation with have also showed progress of EDC in last year and future plans with the help of PowerPoint presentation. There were 250+ students present for the program. The guest of this Program are Suresh shakkarwar, Ashutosh Navendar, Sanjay Shidhye.

Idea Competition

Sep 04th, 2018

The competition was started at 5pm after the inauguration program in the IT seminar hall of our institute. The competition was assessed by faculty coordinator of EDC club Dr. A.M. Idea pitching session in Front of technical Committee (MSME)

Sep 19th, 2018

Our institute is in process of registering for MSME policy. Our institute has to submit at least 10 ideas for Incubation. Pitching session has been arranged for the same in which students presented their ideas in front of college technical committee. Out of 9 teams presenting, 4 teams were selected for incubation recommendation. External Expert for this session was Prashant Deshpande. College technical committee includes departmental HOD and EDC faculty coordinators.
